End of Lease Cleaning

The last thing you want to think about when you’re moving house is the cleaning. But if you want your bond back you’re going to have to exert some effort or save your time and energy and get someone else to do the job for you.

Our End of Lease cleaners are equipped with all the necessary tools, equipment and a powerful enzyme-based cleaning solution.

End of Lease Cleaning Checklist:

Kitchen cleaning
  • All cupboards to be cleaned, the fronts plus inside & outside
  • Fridge to be cleaned inside and out
  • Oven to be cleaned inside & outside
  • Dishwasher to be cleaned inside & Outside
  • Floors to be hovered and moped
  • All inside windows totally washed and doors spot cleaned
  • All skirtings to be dusted and wiped over
  • Light fittings to be dusted and or spot cleaned plus wiped clean
Bathroom cleaning
  • All inside windows and doors to be cleaned
  • Toilet to be cleaned
  • Shower screen and floor
  • Shower head to be de scaled
  • Bath to be cleaned
  • Mirrors to be cleaned
  • Any cupboards and shelves to be cleaned
  • All skirtings to be dusted
  • Light fittings to be dusted and or wiped down
Bedrooms
  • All inside wndows and doors to be cleaned
  • Inside of wardrobes and cupboards to be cleaned
  • Light fittings to be dusted and or wiped over
  • Full vacuum floor or carpets
  • Cleaning of mirrors
  • Cleaning of skirting boards
Lounge
  • All Windows inside and doors to be cleaned
  • Inside of cupboards and shelving units etc
  • Light fittings to be dusted and or wiped over
  • Full vacuum floor or carpet
  • Cleaning of mirrors
  • Cleaning of skirting boards
Hallway and stairs
  • Vacuuming of stairs and hallways
  • Skirting boards to be cleaned
  • Any light fittings to be dusted and or wiped over to remove any marks
