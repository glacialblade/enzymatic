Floor Care

  • Carpet and Carpet Steam Cleaning

    Vacuuming is great for day-to-day carpet maintenance but to keep it at peak performance, professional deep cleaning of carpet every 12 to 18 months is necessary.

    At Enzymatic, we use an enzyme-based formula that dissolves and removes all types of stains and odours, leaves no soapy residue and we use colour-safe bleach for carpets including 5th-generation carpets.

  • Polishing & buffing all hard surfaces

    Beautiful floors can be one of the most impressive features of your facility. Well-maintained floors enhance your professional image and provide a clean, safe working environment.

    Although hard surface floors can be extremely durable, they require proper maintenance to preserve their resilience and luster. Our surface floor cleaning methods, floor care techniques and commercial grade floor waxes will exceed your high standards of cleanliness and floor appearance.

    List of Hard-Surface Floors Services:
    • Floor stripping and waxing
    • Floor scrubbing and refinishing
    • Floor polishing
    • Floor buffing/burnishing
    • Floor maintenance program
